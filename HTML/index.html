How To Train Your Dragon

How To Train Your Dragon is the first instalment of the titular triliogy. Following Hiccup, the misfit heir of his Viking clan, and Toothless, a rare, presumably near extinct dragon that Hiccup befriends.

Characters

The main characters are:

  • Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III
  • Stoik The Vast
  • Gobber The Belch
  • Astrid Hofferson
  • Ruffnut Thorston
  • Tuffnut Thorston
  • Fishlegs Ingerman
  • Snotlout Jorgenson

Fun Facts

  • Toothless' hesitation during the "Touch" scene was initially an animation error that was kept.
  • Toothless' appearance and personality was inspired by cats, dogs, and horses.
  • Animators had to attend “flight school” during production. A legitimate program in which they would study flight physics and movements of different creatures for realism. After graduation, they each even received a diploma.
  • Throughout the film, Hiccup vehemently declares he can’t kill dragons while the other Vikings boast about all the dragons they’ve killed. Ironically, Hiccup is the only Viking we actually see kill a dragon in the whole movie.
  • HTTYD features lots of beards and fur. Not only are beards tough to rig but designing them is also tricky. Stoick’s beard alone took months to make.
