Storyline

*How To Train Your Dragon* takes place on the island of Berk, a Viking settlement where the villagers are constantly at war with dragons. The story follows Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, a young Viking who doesn't fit the mold of a traditional dragon slayer. Instead of being strong and fierce like the other Vikings, Hiccup is small, awkward, and often overlooked by his father, Stoick the Vast, the chief of the village.

Hiccup's life changes when he captures a rare and elusive dragon, a Night Fury. Instead of killing the dragon, as tradition dictates, Hiccup befriends it and names it Toothless. Over time, Hiccup realizes that the dragons are not the bloodthirsty monsters the villagers believe them to be, and he begins to form an unlikely friendship with Toothless. This relationship causes Hiccup to question the traditional Viking view of dragons and sets him on a journey to bridge the gap between humans and dragons.

As the story unfolds, Hiccup becomes a hero in his own right, showing that understanding and compassion can achieve more than brute force. With the help of Toothless, Hiccup leads the charge against a massive dragon, the Red Death, and ultimately helps forge an alliance between the Vikings and the dragons, changing the future of Berk forever.

Main Characters

  • Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III: The main protagonist, Hiccup is a young, awkward Viking who dreams of becoming a dragon slayer but instead befriends a dragon. His intelligence and unique perspective on dragons make him stand out in a world where strength is revered.
  • Toothless: A rare Night Fury dragon, Toothless is initially feared and misunderstood by the Vikings. However, he becomes Hiccup’s closest companion and partner, displaying intelligence and loyalty as they fight side-by-side.
  • Stoick the Vast: Hiccup's father and the chief of Berk. Stoick is a strong and traditional Viking leader who believes that dragons are enemies. He struggles with Hiccup's unconventional approach to dragon hunting and is initially unaware of Hiccup’s bond with Toothless.
  • Astrid Hofferson: A skilled and strong Viking warrior who becomes one of Hiccup's closest allies. Initially skeptical of Hiccup’s abilities, she later joins him in his quest to change the way the village views dragons.
  • Gobber the Belch: A kind-hearted, albeit gruff, blacksmith who trains the young Vikings in dragon hunting. He is a mentor figure to Hiccup and is supportive of his unique way of thinking.

Dragons

  • Toothless: A Night Fury, the rarest and most feared type of dragon. Toothless is initially perceived as a dangerous and destructive creature, but Hiccup forms a special bond with him. He is intelligent, agile, and incredibly loyal.
  • Red Death: The massive dragon that commands the dragon army and is responsible for terrorizing Berk. It is a fearsome creature and is the final antagonist in the film. The Red Death is eventually defeated by Hiccup and Toothless.
  • Stormfly: Astrid's dragon, a Deadly Nadder known for her speed and precision. She is a fierce and loyal companion, often seen battling alongside Astrid during dragon training sessions.
  • Meatlug: A Gronckle dragon, owned by Fishlegs. Meatlug is large, strong, and has a tough exterior but is surprisingly gentle. Despite his tough demeanor, Meatlug is a lovable and loyal dragon who has a special bond with Fishlegs.
  • Hookfang: Snotlout's dragon, a Monstrous Nightmare. Hookfang is a powerful and fiery dragon known for his destructive capabilities. While he shares a brash and often aggressive personality with Snotlout, they have a strong, loyal bond and fight together as a team.
  • Barf and Belch: Twin dragons (a Hideous Zippleback), owned by Tuffnut and Ruffnut. They work in tandem to fight and hunt, displaying a comical but effective teamwork dynamic. The dragons have two heads and are known for their explosive behavior.
